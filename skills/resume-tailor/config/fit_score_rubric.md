# Fit Score Rubric

Updated: 2026-05-04

Fit score is now a weighted signal ledger instead of a flat checklist. Scores still clamp to 1-10, but strong positive fit signals can outweigh small misses, and major mismatch signals can pull a role down even if the title looks close.

## Major positives

| Signal | Weight | Why it matters |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| NYC / nearby location | +3 | Highest location preference for Liam. |
| Early-career, new-grad, junior, associate, Engineer I | +3 | Best level match. |
| 0-2 YOE requirement | +2 | Strong evidence the role is reachable. |
| SWE role family | +2 | Keeps the queue centered on engineering roles. |
| Backend, full-stack, platform, infrastructure, API, cloud | +2 | Strong match to Oracle Cloud / full-stack backend experience. |
| Resume stack match | +2 | Java, Python, TypeScript, React, Node, NestJS, Spring Boot, SQL, cloud, Docker, CI/CD. |
| SF/Bay Area | +2 | Strong secondary geography. |
| High-value target company | +2 | Strategic company preference. |

## Smaller positives

| Signal | Weight | Why it matters |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Remote / hybrid / preferred US location | +1 | Acceptable location, but below NYC and SF. |
| Product/generalist/founding engineer | +1 | Useful breadth match, but less important than level and location. |
| Preferred source | +1 | Ashby, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, or direct company site. |
| Referral/contact present | +1 | Helps prioritization once a verified contact exists. |

## Negatives

| Signal | Weight | Why it matters |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| 3-4 YOE requirement | -2 | Possible reach, but not ideal for Liam's current profile. |
| 5+ YOE requirement | -4 | Major mismatch against early-career positioning. |
| Senior title | -3 | Usually above current level unless otherwise clearly accessible. |
| Staff/principal/manager/director/architect | -5 | Not an outreach/apply priority. |
| Non-SWE role family | -4 | Prevents sales, recruiting, analyst, and similar roles from ranking too high. |
| Staffing/recruiting company | -3 | Lower-quality target unless there is a direct role reason. |
| Clearance / citizenship hard gate | -3 | Potential eligibility blocker. |
| Rejected/archived status | -8 | Should not remain in active reach-out priority. |
